FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Curiosity cracks million dollar problems.

What route yields the best results in the software development space?

You have a spectrum of options—from diving into no-code platforms solo, to partnering with a no-code agency for speed and top-notch results, or opting for traditional development. Regardless of your path, never lose sight of the paramount importance of user experience.


Our promise? Delivering a fully functioning app without breaking the bank compared to traditional methods. Our goal lies in creating visually stunning applications, finely tuned for optimal user interaction and satisfaction.

How can I build and launch software?

Leonidaz is renowned for its proficiency in crafting tailor-made applications. With an unmatched ability to deliver MVPs in under a month, it consistently outpaces traditional development approaches, all while substantially cutting costs. This remarkable achievement is realized through a combination of low-code development and coding structures and frameworks. Leonidaz is the go-to choice for startups and established corporations aiming to swiftly conceptualize, build, and successfully launch their software idea.

I have a software idea, how can I mitigate my risk and launch it successfully?

Start by figuring out what your long-term goals are. This will determine the longevity of your pursuit in the industry. Then, come up with ideas and enhance them by adding value. Follow that with a plan on how users can benefit from your app. You can either hire a company to build your platform or do it yourself with software. Finally, launch the basic version of your product and track how people use it to keep improving.

How involved can I be in the development process?

We highly value client engagement! We maintain constant communication throughout the process, offering frequent updates and actively seeking your input. Your contributions are essential in shaping the end result.

What tools does Leonidaz primarily use?

Our primary software suite operates primarily on Bubble. We integrate code, including HTML, CSS, and JS, both for the UI/UX and backend, to elevate its functionality. To seamlessly link everything together, we rely on Zapier for APIs and similar tools to ensure optimal software performance.

Leonidaz was built on years of expertise, driven by a passion for innovation, and a relentless pursuit of excellence in the software space.
